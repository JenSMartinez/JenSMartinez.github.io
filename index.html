My name is Jen...



Nice to meet you! I enlisted in the U.S. Marine Corps out of San Diego in 2010. During my time as a military photojournalist, I served in Fort Worth Texas, Fort Meade, Maryland, Syracuse, New York and Okinawa, Japan. In 2017, I graduated from the Military Visual Journalism Program at S.I. Newhouse School of Communications, Syracuse University. In 2018 I was selected as a content team lead for the Department of Defense' redesign project for Defense.gov. I am currently persuing a B.A. in Journalism/ Social Media at University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
