About Me

Jen enlisted in the U.S. Marine Corps out of San Diego in 2010. During her time as a military photojournalist, she served in Fort Worth Texas, Fort Meade, Maryland, Syracuse, New York and Okinawa, Japan. In 2017, She graduated from the Military Visual Journalism Program at S.I. Newhouse School of Communications, Syracuse University. In 2018 she was selected as a content team lead for the Department of Defense' redesign project for Defense.gov. She is currently persuing a B.A. in Journalism/ Social Media at University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
